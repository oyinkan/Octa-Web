Frequently Asked Questions

About Octa/General

What is Octa?

Octa is a web based and mobile app channel that helps you invest and save simultaneously. With Octa you get up to 12% interest on your savings and 18% on your investments. Octa helps you invest and save towards a goal or target.

How do I start investing and saving with Octa?

To begin investing and saving, download and register on the app or our website and fund your investment account.

How do I set up a savings account?

Click “+ start savings” on the dashboard or menu tab, provide the required information. You can also view the tour guide to see steps for each action.

What document do I need to register for octa?

You don’t need any document. You only need the app.

Why use Octa?

Octa is a smart solution that helps you invest your money and simultaneously save the earned returns on your investment. With Octa, you earn above bank interest rate on both savings and investments. You also enjoy a smart and convenient approach to savings and investments.

How much does it cost to use the Octa app?

Using the app is completely free. We do not charge for SMS, withdrawals or transactions. Everything is completely free.

Savings and Interest

How much can I start with?

You don’t need millions to start saving. With Octa, you can save as little as N1000 and be on your way to smart financial growth.

What is the interest rate?

You earn up to 12% as interest on your regular savings (Auto save).

When is interest paid?

Interest is calculated and paid into your wallet monthly.

Can I set up more than one savings goal?

Yes, you can. You can create different savings for different goals and even give each one a unique name for easy recall.

Can I lock my account?

Octa does not currently have a lock feature. We believe you should be free to determine what you want to do with your money and we also want to gain your trust.

How long is maturity?

That is entirely up to you. However, keep in mind that the longer you save, the more your interest is.

Security

How safe is my money?

Your money is very safe. All funds are housed by Page International Financial Services Limited, a registered and licensed retail financial service provider in Nigeria.

What is the relationship between Octa and Page Financials?

Page International Financial Services is the holding company of Octa. Page International Financial Services is a licensed financial institution in Nigeria, so Octa is fully covered and regulated.

Are my card details safe?

Yes, your card details are very safe, we work with Remita to process all payments.

I can’t remember my security question

You can change this. Sign into your account, visit the profile section and update or change your security question and answer.

Why are you requesting for my BVN?

CBN requires you to have a BVN to own a fully functional account. We need your BVN to automatically gather your personal information, so you won't need to fill any form when signing up.

What if I don’t have funds in my bank account?

We will try to deduct from your source account twice. If deduction is not possible, it would be left. Subsequent deductions will continue thereafter.
